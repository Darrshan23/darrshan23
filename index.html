About Our Team

Evolved Hackers Club is a collective of cybersecurity enthusiasts, ethical hackers, and digital defenders dedicated to pushing the boundaries of security knowledge and practice. We believe in using our skills for positive impact, education, and innovation in the cybersecurity landscape.

Our team is built on the principles of collaboration, continuous learning, and ethical responsibility. We actively participate in security research, vulnerability disclosure, and cybersecurity competitions to hone our skills and contribute to a safer digital world.

We are proud to announce our participation in the Curtin Malaysia Capture The Flag (CTF) competition. This event represents an opportunity to test our skills against real-world challenges and connect with the wider cybersecurity community.
